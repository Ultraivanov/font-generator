Brockmann Style Checklist

  • O, C, G — perfect circles (squarish feel)
  • H, N, U — stems at consistent width
  • M — apex at optical center
  • K, R — legs meet at consistent angle
  • S — symmetric curves, horizontal terminals
  • a, e, s — open apertures
  • x-height ~72-74% of cap height
  • Monolinear stroke (no contrast)
  • Vertical stress (no slant in roman)
  • Minimal rounding (function over decoration)

Issues to Review

  • S — check curve balance and terminal alignment
  • C, G — verify aperture openness vs. consistency
  • M — confirm apex position and width
  • K — check leg angles and junction point
  • R — verify leg position and curve
  • k — ensure leg angles match K proportions
  • s — mirror of S, check balance
  • Spacing consistency across rounds (O, C, G, D, Q)
  • Italic slant angle (should be ~9-11°)
